About Me

My name is Manuel Colino Fernández, and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Aerospace Engineering at ISAE-SUPAERO. I come from Spain, where I completed most of my Bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. During my third year, I had the opportunity to study in the United States at Purdue University, an experience that greatly broadened my international perspective and shaped the way I approach engineering and teamwork.

In total, I have accumulated around nine months of internship experience — three months in Spain and six months in Germany — which I completed during the final year of my Bachelor's studies. I have now been living in Toulouse for over a year, where I am continuing my studies at Supaero and starting my specialization in Systems Engineering.

On a more personal level, I am a sociable and curious person, who enjoys working in teams and meeting new people from different backgrounds. I am passionate about outdoor activities and sports such as cycling, motorbiking, and running, and since arriving in Toulouse, I have had the chance to begin my Private Pilot License (PPL) training — a dream I have had since childhood and one that perfectly connects with my passion for aerospace.
